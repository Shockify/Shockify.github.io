FAQ




will the game be free?

To keep it sort and sweet, we don't know. Our team at Nidir are working and fousing on the game first before getting into how pricing will be played out.

when can other players access it?

We'll keep you updated. We're going to have different phases depending on what's currently happening in the development. Sometimes, we might allow streamers/youtubers to hop on or we might invite all. Join the email newsletter, and we can send you an email when we're ready!

When will all bugs be fixed

During development stages, we'll have mass game sessions (stress test) in which players will be able to submit bug requests. Before going forward on the next update, ALL bugs should and will be checked and fixed before updating to the newest version.

As of now, while in development stages, there is no downloadable button. The only way to access this game is from the people inside Nadir.

Can i get the download to test?

YES, We will post downloadable content on a seperate unlisted link, in which only people who are in the newsletter will receive.
